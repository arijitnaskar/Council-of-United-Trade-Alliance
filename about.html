About the Council of United Trade Alliance

A collective platform for experienced MSME leaders, manufacturers, and entrepreneurs committed to practical progress and stronger enterprise representation.

Who we are

Experience brought together with purpose.

The Council of United Trade Alliance is a collective of MSME industry leaders, manufacturers, traders, service providers, and entrepreneurs across West Bengal. It was formed to bring experienced business voices onto one platform where industry knowledge, administrative engagement, and enterprise development can move forward with purpose.

Our members carry hands-on experience in manufacturing, supply chain management, local trade, procurement, compliance, and commercial operations. That experience gives the Council a practical understanding of regulatory complexity, tender participation, documentation pressure, market access, and operational uncertainty.

Through awareness programmes, engagement with administrative bodies, and member-to-member networking, we aim to strengthen the competitiveness and confidence of local enterprises.

Collective insight.Turning individual experience into shared knowledge and constructive action.
Our mission

Champion MSME interests.

To improve market visibility, enable access to developmental resources, encourage practical compliance awareness, and build a collaborative network that helps local enterprises become stronger, more resilient, and more competitive.

Our vision

Connect enterprise and opportunity.

To become a trusted, active, and respected MSME alliance that contributes to West Bengal's industrial progress by connecting enterprise, knowledge, policy dialogue, and opportunity.

Our objectives

From shared concerns to useful action.

  • Represent common interests of MSME manufacturers, business owners, and entrepreneurs.
  • Create structured dialogue with government departments, institutions, and industry stakeholders.
  • Build awareness around taxation, documentation, tendering, procurement, and compliance.
  • Promote fair and accessible business opportunities for local MSME units.
  • Encourage member collaboration, referrals, knowledge-sharing, and joint initiatives.
  • Keep members informed about relevant schemes, notices, and developmental resources.
