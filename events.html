18Jan 2024

GST Awareness Programme at Shilpa Bhavan

Shilpa Bhavan, Kolkata · 60+ participants

The Council organised a focused GST awareness programme to support MSMEs with practical tax and regulatory guidance. More than 60 business owners, manufacturers, and industry representatives participated.

The event was inaugurated by Mr. Nikhil Nirmal, IAS, Managing Director of WBSIDCL. The technical session was conducted by Mr. Rajat Subhra Paul, Deputy Commissioner of Commercial Taxes.

Session focus

  • Recent GST updates for manufacturing and trading supply chains
  • Online facilities and common filing practices
  • Documentation discipline and common compliance errors
  • Input tax credit and interstate trade documentation
  • Audit preparedness and member Q&A
08Mar 2025

Delegation Meeting at Nabanna

State Secretariat, Howrah · Policy engagement

A senior Council delegation met Dr. Manoj Pant, IAS, Chief Secretary of West Bengal, to discuss practical concerns around WBSIDCL work distribution and the state's e-tendering system.

The objective was to make procurement participation more transparent, streamlined, and accessible for experienced local MSME units.

Discussion areas

  • Balanced and time-bound work distribution
  • Simpler and standardised documentation
  • Reduced administrative barriers in e-tendering
  • Predictable access to government-linked opportunities
  • Stronger participation by local manufacturers
