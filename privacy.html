Privacy Policy

How information submitted through this website may be used and protected.

This is a concise website policy based on the supplied draft and should be reviewed by the Council before formal adoption.

Information we receive

The Council may receive information that visitors voluntarily provide through email links, membership enquiries, contact forms, event communication, or direct correspondence. This can include contact details, organisation details, and the content of an enquiry.

How information may be used

Submitted information may be used for membership processing, event communication, official notices, enquiry response, Council-related updates, and maintaining accurate member records.

Information sharing

The Council will take reasonable steps to protect submitted information and will not intentionally share personal information with unrelated third parties without a proper reason, consent, or legal requirement.

Your responsibility

Do not submit sensitive personal, financial, tax, or identity documents through a general enquiry unless specifically requested through an approved channel by the Council Secretariat.

External websites

This website contains links to external websites. The Council is not responsible for the privacy practices, security, availability, or content of those websites.

Contact

Questions about this policy may be sent to unitedtradeallince@gmail.com.
