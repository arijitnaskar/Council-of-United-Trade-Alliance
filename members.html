Membership at CUTA

Be informed, connected, and represented.

Membership is open to eligible business owners, MSME units, entrepreneurs, manufacturers, traders, service providers, institutions, and professionals who share the Council's commitment to constructive enterprise development.

As a member, you become part of a serious business platform focused on practical awareness, networking, representation, and access to relevant information. Active participation helps the Council understand industry concerns and take them forward in a structured manner.

Member benefits

  • MSME-focused seminars and awareness programmes
  • Relevant notices, schemes, meetings, and updates
  • Connections with experienced enterprise leaders
  • A collective forum for practical business concerns
  • Visibility through approved member listings and activities
  • A platform that values responsible business conduct
Membership categories

A place for every enterprise contributor.

Final category terms, fees, and approval conditions are governed by the Council's official rules.

General Member

For MSME owners, proprietors, partners, directors, and entrepreneurs who wish to participate in Council activities.

Associate Member

For professionals, consultants, advisors, and service providers connected with MSME development.

Corporate / Institutional

For companies, trade bodies, associations, and institutions engaging with the Council's MSME network.

Honorary Member

For eminent persons and industry contributors invited by the Council in recognition of their support or guidance.

How to apply

A clear five-step process.

The Secretariat may request additional information or supporting documents before an application is considered.

1

Complete the application

Provide your personal, business, contact, and preferred membership details.

2

Prepare supporting documents

Keep identity proof, business registration, Udyam, GST, and other applicable records ready.

3

Submit to the Secretariat

Send the application online, by email, or submit it in person as instructed.

4

Application review

The authorised office bearers or membership committee may contact you for clarification.

5

Confirmation

Following approval and payment of applicable fees, membership confirmation is issued.

Eligibility

Before you apply.

  • Be connected with business, manufacturing, trade, services, entrepreneurship, professional practice, or MSME development.
  • Agree to follow the Council's rules, code of conduct, and membership conditions.
  • Provide accurate details, documents, and declarations where requested.
  • Understand that approval is subject to review by the Council's authorised representatives.
Code of conduct

Responsible participation.

  • Maintain respectful conduct in Council meetings, events, and communications.
  • Do not misuse the Council's name, logo, letterhead, or communication channels.
  • Do not represent the Council externally without formal authorisation.
  • Follow applicable laws, ethical commercial practices, and membership requirements.
