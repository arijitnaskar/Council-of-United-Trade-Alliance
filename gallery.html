January 18, 2024 Shilpa Bhavan, Kolkata

GST Awareness Programme

A focused awareness and interaction programme organised to help MSME representatives strengthen their understanding of GST procedures, documentation, filing practices, and day-to-day compliance responsibilities.

Through technical discussion, attentive participation, and direct exchange, the session created a practical forum for members to raise operational questions and gain greater clarity.

02
March 8, 2025 Nabanna, Howrah

Delegation Meets the Chief Secretary

A CUTA delegation met Shri Manoj Pant, IAS, Chief Secretary of West Bengal, at his office in Nabanna to discuss important issues affecting the MSME sector.

The delegation also sought support for recognising the distinct needs of the tiny enterprise segment within the revised MSME framework. The representation received a patient hearing, with assurance that the concerns would be considered urgently.

Direct dialogue with the administration gives ground-level enterprise concerns a clear and constructive voice.

March 16, 2026 Shilpa Bhavan, Kolkata

Welcoming R. Vimal, IAS, at Shilpa Bhavan

On March 16, 2026, CUTA extended a warm welcome to R. Vimal, IAS, Managing Director of the West Bengal Small Industries Development Corporation Limited (WBSIDCL), at Shilpa Bhavan.

The interaction reflected the Council's commitment to maintaining constructive institutional relationships and strengthening dialogue around the development, participation, and practical needs of MSMEs in West Bengal.

Recent MSME Engagements

Mr. Anirban Bhattacharya, President of CUTA, met Shri Asok Dinda, Minister of State, MSME, to discuss practical issues and concerns faced by micro, small, and medium enterprises.

CUTA representatives also discussed MSME development in rural areas with Shri Dilip Ghosh, Hon'ble Minister-in-Charge, Panchayats and Rural Development, with focus on strengthening grassroots enterprise opportunities.
